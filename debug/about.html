About Cascade Forge

Developer infrastructure, shipped at the speed of your team.

Who we are

Cascade Forge is a privately held software company headquartered in Vancouver, British Columbia. Founded in 2018 by Maya Chen and Daniel Park, the company designs and operates developer tools that shorten the feedback loop between writing code and learning whether it works in production.

Cascade Forge is trusted by over 1,400 engineering teams across 42 countries, with offices in Vancouver, Berlin, and Singapore.

Compliance and audit

Since January 2024, Cascade Forge has partnered with Arrowpath Assurance as its independent SOC 2 audit firm. Arrowpath conducts the annual SOC 2 Type II audit and reviews Cascade Forge's control environment on a quarterly basis.

Cascade Forge additionally holds ISO 27001 certification, originally issued in November 2024 and renewed annually.

Products

  • Helix CI — distributed continuous integration for monorepos.
  • PulseDB — managed time-series database for metrics and event data.
  • Stitch SDK — vendor-neutral observability SDK with OpenTelemetry support.
