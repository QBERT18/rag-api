# Cascade Forge — Company Overview

Cascade Forge is a privately held software company headquartered in Vancouver, British Columbia. The company designs and operates developer infrastructure tools used by engineering teams to ship software faster and observe it in production.

## Founding

Cascade Forge was founded in April 2018 by Maya Chen and Daniel Park. Maya previously led the build platform team at a large cloud provider, and Daniel was an early engineer at two infrastructure startups. The two met while contributing to an open-source build cache project in 2015.

The company started in a co-working space in Gastown with a team of four. The first commercial product, **Helix CI**, was released to private beta in November 2018 and reached general availability in May 2019.

## Mission

The company's mission is to **shorten the feedback loop between writing code and learning whether it works**. Every product Cascade Forge ships is measured against one of three internal metrics: time-to-first-build, time-to-first-trace, and time-to-resolved-incident.

## Offices

Cascade Forge maintains three offices:

1. **Vancouver, BC (HQ)** — engineering, product, finance. Roughly 70 staff.
2. **Berlin, Germany** — opened January 2022. EMEA sales and support. Roughly 25 staff.
3. **Singapore** — opened September 2024. APAC sales and a small platform team. Roughly 15 staff.

A remote-first policy applies to all engineering roles. As of Q1 2026 the company employs **127 people** across 18 countries.
